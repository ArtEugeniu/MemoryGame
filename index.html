Memory Game

Improve your memory!

Rules

  • You need to remember the numbers in eight cells.
  • The numbers range from 1 to 8 and are placed randomly.
  • Your task is to open the cells in the correct order, from 1 to 8.
  • If you select the wrong number once, you get another chance.
  • If you make a second mistake, the game resets, and you have to start over.
